About

Founded in 1948, Flash Recruitment has a long history of connecting opportunities at great companies with highly skilled job seekers.

Bolstered by the strength of our brand, our people, our technology and our professional business model, we find meaningful and exciting employment for the people we place and provide clients access to the specialised talent they need to help grow.

We pioneered the idea of professional talent solutions nearly 70 years ago and, as the needs of businesses have evolved, so have we.
