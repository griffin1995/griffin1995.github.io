  1. FAQ
  2. Will FR send my CV to clients without contacting me?
  3. No NEVER! We only present your details to a client after we have spoken to you about the vacancy, the company and received your permission to do so.

  4. I have applied for a vacancy and I have not been contacted- why is this?
  5. We acknowledge all applications via e-mail, if we think that you are suitable for the role then we will contact you to discuss the role within 2 working days. If we have not contacted you by then it could be that we felt you were unsuitable for the role or that we have not received your CV. Either way, feel free to get in touch with us and we will be more than happy to chat through and discuss.

  6. I have sent a copy of my CV and I can see vacancies on your website that I think I am suitable for. Why have you not called me?
  7. There could be a number of reasons for this. Sometimes our clients are very specific about what they are looking for. For example, they might have told us that they only want to see candidates with certain industry experience or background, or a specific skill set. It could also be based on the information we have on your personal file. Ultimately though, if there is a position that we know you would like and we can put you forward for, rest assured we will!
